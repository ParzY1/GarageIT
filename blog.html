Fixing two new DNSSEC vulnerabilities

Today, we have been informed about two DNSSEC vulnerabilities in dnsmasq, which Pi-hole FTL is forked from. Both vulnerabilities, via specially crafted DNSSEC answers, can lead DNSSEC validators down a very CPU intensive and time costly validation/NSEC3 hash calculation path. This results in degraded performance and denial of service in trivially orchestrated attacks.
